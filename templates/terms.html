A

Accreditation

The formal process of recognizing a diplomat as an official representative of their country.

Alliance

A formal agreement or treaty between two or more nations to cooperate for specific purposes.

B

Back-channel Diplomacy

Secret or unofficial communication channels used to facilitate sensitive negotiations.

Balance of Power

A theory in international relations suggesting that national security is enhanced when military capability is distributed so that no one state is strong enough to dominate all others.
