About the Project

Our mission is to provide a clear, modern, and accessible resource for the language of global affairs.

Our Mission

In an increasingly complex and interconnected world, understanding the language of diplomacy is more critical than ever. DIPLOMACY.IO was created to demystify the terminology used by diplomats, scholars, and political analysts. We aim to be a living resource that evolves with the landscape of international relations, incorporating new concepts from cyber, environmental, and public diplomacy.
