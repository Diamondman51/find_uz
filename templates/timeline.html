Timeline of Diplomatic Concepts

Trace the historical evolution of key terms and events in international relations.

1648

Westphalian Sovereignty

The Peace of Westphalia establishes the principle of state sovereignty, where each state has exclusive control over its territory.

1815

Concert of Europe

Following the Napoleonic Wars, major European powers create a system of consultation (or "congress system") to maintain a balance of power.

1945

Multilateralism

The founding of the United Nations institutionalizes the practice of international cooperation among multiple states to solve global problems.

1991

Track II Diplomacy

The term is popularized by Joseph Montville to describe non-official conflict resolution efforts, gaining prominence after the Cold War.
